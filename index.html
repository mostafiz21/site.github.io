ABOUT Dgensys

DGENSYS LLC is an international import, export and wholesale company based out of the United States.Our core business consists of importing textile oriented products from South Asia and distributing them in the USA, Europe and Africa. We also export food products like Soybean oil, Soy, Sunflower oil. We have been able to develop strong direct relationships with many manufacturers in Bangladesh. These relationships allow us to control and more closely monitor the manufacturing process and further ensure high quality and timely shipments.To accommodate all of our international trade, DGENSYS has a satellite office in Bangladesh. Through our office, we are able to expedite deliveries to anywhere in the world.
